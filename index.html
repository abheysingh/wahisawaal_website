Releasing this Summer

 A short fiction film
dealing with the issue of scars left on a child due to growing up in an abusive household.




Synopsis

A boy who went through a rough childhood mainly because of the abusive relationship between his parents.

His father is well educated but belongs to a rural family, who in his own childhood was not well treated. mother comes from a poor family, married at the age of 18, she hardens over the time changing from a dreamy young girl to a hard, bitter middle age woman.

The child gets distorted over time witnessing domestic violence again and again. unable to get any mental support he becomes lonelier and lonelier over time. growing older he tries to distance himself from his past, tucking it away in the dark corners of his heart.

But one day it all snaps open, leaving him helpless.
